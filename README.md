# Strategic Growth Assessment of Reliance Industries Ltd.

# Overview

This project presents a strategic business analysis of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) using Microsoft Excel and Microsoft PowerPoint. The objective was to analyze the company's business performance, visualize key financial indicators, and present strategic insights through an executive dashboard and presentation.



# Project Objectives

- Analyze the business segments of Reliance Industries Ltd.
- Study revenue trends across financial years.
- Visualize business segment contributions.
- Present key company metrics through KPI cards.
- Summarize findings in a professional presentation.



# Dashboard Features

- Revenue Trend Analysis (FY21–FY25)
- Business Contribution Pie Chart
-  KPI Cards
  - Revenue
  - Market Capitalization
  - Employees
  - Fortune Global Rank
- Executive dashboard layout designed in Microsoft Excel



# Presentation Highlights

The PowerPoint presentation includes:

- Company Overview
- Business Segment Analysis
- Jio Platforms
- Reliance Retail
- Oil-to-Chemicals (O2C)
- New Energy
- Financial Performance
- SWOT Analysis
- Strategic Recommendations
- Conclusion

# Key Insights

- Revenue shows consistent growth from FY21 to FY25.
- Oil-to-Chemicals remains the largest business segment.
- Retail and Digital Services contribute significantly to overall business.
- New Energy represents an emerging growth opportunity.
